ABOUT

Studio Khuna is a local brand based in Jakarta, Indonesia. We over at Khuna prioritize quality while maintaining slick and fashionable styles to our apparel. We design and pick our fabrics by hand, making sure of the quality our products.

Khuna was started in 2022 as a creative outlet for future project to come. As time went on, Khuna evolved from a concept to a fashion brand. Our passion resides in street-wear aesthetic - we work with that concept in mind making sure to bestow the best look and feel for our audience.
